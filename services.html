Unique Matching Method

We go through an extensive matching process to make sure our dogs are matched with their appropriate companions. We begin learning each dog's personality at birth and make exhaustive efforts to complement their match's personality. No stone is left unturned and we even discuss the person's sleep schedule to compare to the dog's behavior regarding sleep.

 

Extensive Training Using Social Skills

As we have the dog and their new owner interact together, we put an emphasis of the use of social skills. Greetings, saying goodbye, behaving in public, walking together, and being able to read each other are just a few of the skills that we focus on. This also helps with matching a person to the appropriate companion.

 

Having Fun is the Key!

If your dog is happy and wanting to play, it is sure to ease someone's anxiety or depression. Our dogs "treat" their owner's symptoms with unconditional love, nurturing, and by continuing to strengthen the bond between themselves and their owner. They truly desire to "heal" their owner in their own way and being playful and lighthearted is so important. Our customers often contact us to let us know the happiness their dog is bringing them.
